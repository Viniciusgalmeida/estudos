# Mission

## Why

Become a senior developer with strong system design fluency. Reading The Pragmatic Programmer to internalize the mindsets, habits, and practices that distinguish senior engineers from mid-level ones.

## Success Criteria

- Can apply pragmatic thinking to daily coding decisions without consciously consulting a list of tips
- Can identify and name anti-patterns in codebases and articulate why they're harmful
- Can advocate for technical quality decisions using principled reasoning, not just intuition

## Constraints

- Learning chapter by chapter, depth over speed
- Goal is behavior change, not just conceptual familiarity

## Out of Scope

- Project management / scrum ceremonies
- Business/sales topics unrelated to engineering craft
