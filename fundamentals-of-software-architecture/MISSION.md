# Mission: Fundamentals of Software Architecture

## Why
Become a senior developer with strong system design skills. Understanding how architects think — their expectations, trade-offs, and decisions — will directly improve the quality of design choices made day-to-day as a developer, and build the foundation needed to grow into senior and eventually architect-level roles.

## Success looks like
- Able to reason about architectural trade-offs in real projects (not just name patterns)
- Understand what separates a senior developer's design thinking from a junior's
- Can discuss system design decisions with architects and contribute meaningfully
- Complete Fundamentals of Software Architecture (2020) with retained, applicable knowledge

## Constraints
- Learning through the book chapter by chapter (currently Ch. 1, p. 28)
- Wants depth, not speed-run — retention over coverage

## Out of scope
- Deep dive into specific cloud providers or DevOps tooling
- Management or people-leadership topics beyond what the book covers
